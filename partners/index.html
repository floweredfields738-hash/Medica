White Hair Pro

New partnership for aesthetic and beauty clinics.

White Hair Pro is a partnership opportunity for aesthetic and beauty clinics wanting to offer the latest and most advanced hair removal treatments. Launching in the UK and EU in April 2026, we are now accepting registrations to become a hair removal specialist.

Our exclusive breakthrough technology provides permanent hair removal of any hair, including white, grey, blonde and red. There is no other device in the world today that can offer safe, effective, permanent hair removal for all hair colours. This painless therapy is suitable for any skin type.
