### 6.5 A New Approach to Hair Removal

**Problem:**
"Conventional laser hair removal systems rely on pigment to target the hair follicle. As a result, they struggle to treat light-coloured hair such as white, grey, blonde or red hair."

"Many laser technologies also cannot be used safely over tattooed skin, leaving a large group of patients with limited treatment options."

**Previous solution:**
"For these patients, electrolysis has traditionally been the only effective solution – but it is slow, labour-intensive and often impractical for larger treatment areas."

**New solution:**
"White Hair Pro introduces a new alternative."
"The system enables clinics to treat all hair colours and all skin types, including white and grey hair, while also allowing treatment over tattooed areas."

### 6.9 How White Hair Pro Works

"White Hair Pro uses a combination of LED light energy and high-frequency waves to target the hair follicle. This process delivers controlled microcurrents to the follicle via the hair shaft, gradually weakening the hair bulb and reducing its ability to produce new hair over time."

"Unlike traditional laser systems, the technology does not rely on pigment, allowing it to treat light-coloured hair effectively. In addition, while electrolysis works through chemical-thermal destruction, White Hair Pro progressively reduces follicle activity until the hair is permanently removed, while protecting the surrounding skin."

### 6.10 Key Advantages

- Effective treatment of white, grey, blonde and red hair
- Safe treatment over tattooed skin
- Permanent hair removal results
- Faster treatment for large areas compared with electrolysis
- Suitable for all skin types
- Low discomfort for patients
